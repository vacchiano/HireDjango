About

I built HireDjango to try and fix the issues job seekers and hiring managers face throughout the process. I've been on both sides of the spectrum and neither is too enjoyable. Developers fire off endless proposals or applications and many times never receive a reply. Business' are flooded with applicants, many of whom send over a very generic resume or proposal. Both sides waste unnecessary time early on for projects or jobs that aren't a good fit. HireDjango aims to fix that. Developers make a free profile and add preferences and experiences. Companies can browse these devs and reach out to those that fit their criteria. The convo starts a step ahead of generic job listings or blanket proposals.
